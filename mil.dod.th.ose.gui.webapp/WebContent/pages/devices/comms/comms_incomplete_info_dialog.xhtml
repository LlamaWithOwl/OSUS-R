An incomplete stack means that not all information for a stack was available at the time the page was rendered. This could be due to a loss in data communications, an invalid or nonexistent layer value, or the page simply tried to retrieve stack information before it was completely set.

If the issue is a case of the last scenario, a simple Page Refresh should fix the problem. Otherwise, the stack information displayed may be invalid until the required information is received or the given information is corrected.
